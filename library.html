📋 How to paste into Excel

  1. Open your Capital Works .xlsm file in Excel
  2. Go to Summary Page for Building Data
  3. Click cell A on your first empty row below existing items
  4. Press Ctrl + V to paste
  5. Column F (Value) will be blank — drag the =C*E formula down from the row above
  6. Click your Run button to recalculate
